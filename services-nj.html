Lawn Care Tips for New Jersey Homeowners

Best Time to Mow: Mow when grass is dry, typically mid-morning or late afternoon. Avoid mowing during the hottest part of the day.

Proper Mowing Height: Keep grass at 2.5-3.5 inches tall. Taller grass develops deeper roots and crowds out weeds naturally.

Seasonal Care: New Jersey lawns benefit from spring aeration, summer watering, fall overseeding, and proper winter preparation.

Weed Control: Early spring pre-emergent treatments prevent crabgrass. Late spring and fall applications control broadleaf weeds.
